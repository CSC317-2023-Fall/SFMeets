Frequently Asked Questions

What is the purpose of the Website?

The purpose of our website is to bring together people who want to create an event or a travel group to an event in SFSU. It is basically going to be a clone of Gator eXperience but with our spin of it.

How do I create an organization?

To create an organization, go to the organization tab and click create an organization and fill out the name and description of your organization.

How do I join an event?

To join an event, go to the events tab and click join on the event you are interested in joining.
